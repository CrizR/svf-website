Get Involved

By joining the Fund, you will become part of a community that will cultivate your intellect, hone your passions, and drive your professional ambitions. Our organization actively seeks to foster personal growth within our members while offering a social setting of like-minded people. We accept recruits at the beginning of each academic semester. All majors and years are encouraged to apply during specified application windows. For consideration to become an Analyst, please attach your resume and an investment pitch (examples below) for submission. Including any financial modelling is not required but highly preferred. If our executive board deems you to be a potential good fit, you will be contacted by the Fund to schedule an interview after which a decision on your candidacy will be made. Attend our informational meeting for more details or e-mail us with any questions at northeastern.svf@gmail.com
